# Air-Quality-Forcasting-using-ML

Air pollution is one of the major health hazards in rapid urbanizing areas. Especially,
fine particulate matter, which is smaller than 2.5 micrometers in diameter (PM2.5), possess more severe health risk compared to other pollutants. Here, I have modeled the
concentration levels of PM2.5 across four forecasting horizons (1, 6, 12 and 24 hours) using four machine learning models, namely XGBoost, CatBoost, LightGBM and Random
Forest.
